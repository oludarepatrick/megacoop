Who We Are

Mega (Ikeja) Cooperative Multipurpose Society Limited (MegaCoop) was launched in 2017 and formally registered with the Lagos State Ministry of Commerce, Industry and Cooperatives in December 2018 following an official application on January 17, 2018. The Cooperative comprises of a variety of resourceful members who desire to belong to a community of investors and collectively grow their knowledge and experience in personal investments towards attaining financial security.
